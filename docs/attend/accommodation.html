Accomodation

While the campus of Université Grenoble Alpes, and therefore the conference location, is outside the city center, we recommend staying in the city center and to go to the conference venue by Tram (15-20min journey, see Venue).

Some of these hotels are offering a special rate for attendees. Please check the dropdown information and use the appropriate code, CERE25 or CAMP-CERE2025, depending on the hotel.
